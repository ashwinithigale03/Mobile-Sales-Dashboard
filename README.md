# 📱 Mobile-Sales-Dashboard

### 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze mobile sales performance across multiple dimensions such as revenue, product categories, customer behavior, and time-based trends.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to track KPIs, identify top-performing products, and understand sales patterns, helping in data-driven decision-making for business growth.

### 📊 Key Insights from the Dashboard

- 📈 Total Revenue: $769M
- 📦 Units Sold: 19K+
- 🧾 Transactions: 4K+
- 💲 Avg Order Value: ~$40

- Premium smartphone segment contributes the highest revenue
- Sales peak during festive seasons and promotional periods
- Online channels outperform offline sales in total transactions
- Certain brands dominate both revenue and unit sales
- Accessories category shows high volume but lower revenue contribution

### 📌 Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing and inventory on top-performing mobile models to maximize revenue and profitability.
- Leverage peak sales periods (monthly/seasonal trends) by running targeted promotions and discount campaigns.
- Strengthen digital payment offers (UPI, cards) such as cashback and EMI options to boost conversions.
- Expand presence in high-performing metro cities while exploring growth opportunities in emerging markets.
- Improve products with average customer ratings by analyzing feedback and enhancing features or service quality.
- Promote mid-range and low-performing products through bundling, discounts, or targeted marketing strategies.
- Use day-wise sales patterns to optimize campaign timing and staffing for better operational efficiency.


### 🧠 Business Questions Answered

- Which mobile brands generate the highest revenue?
- What are the best-selling and least-selling products?
- How do sales vary across months and seasons?
- Which sales channels perform better (online vs offline)?
- What is the average order value and customer purchase behavior?
